Evacuation Procedures

As overseers, please be familiar with the Hall's evacuation procedures. The Platform and Attendant overseers will need to pay special attention, because you are designated wardens.

A video has been lovingly prepared by our Assembly Hall Committee and can be found at this website: http://ca1bv.jwapp.xyz. You may share that URL to your volunteers, especially to the Attendants.

Volunter Pool - Brothers

The Google Sheet below contains a list of brothers provided by the congregations secretaries from our Circuit. You may contact the brother or congregation if you would like to recruit any into your departments. The list is a guide only, and does not mean you are restricted from recruiting other brothers not on this list. Of course, please make sure they are qualified.

The names in Gray background are trained in the Audio-Video Department and are not available before and during the session, and likely will have limited availability after the session. Please consider this when double-booking these brothers as volunteers.

This Google Sheet is not publicly accessible, but is shared using the email address used in the department contact list. If your listed email address is non-Gmail, but you own a Gmail account and prefer to use these for shared documents, please provide your Gmail account to the Assembly Overseer.
