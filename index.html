Emergency Procedures

As overseers, please be familiar with the Hall's emergency procedures. The Platform and Attendant overseers will need to give special attention because you are designated wardens in an event of an emergency.

All overseers and assistants are encouraged to familiarise themselves with the Hall's emergency manual: click here to download the manual.

Also, a video has been prepared by our Assembly Hall Committee and can be found at this location: http://ca1bv.jwapp.xyz. You may share that URL to all your volunteers, especially to the Attendants.

Volunter Pool - Brothers

The Google Sheet below contains a list of brothers provided by the congregations secretaries from our Circuit. You may contact the brother or congregation if you would like to recruit any into your departments. The list is a guide only, and does not mean you are restricted from recruiting other brothers not on this list. Of course, please make sure they are qualified.

As you recruit volunteers, please update the "Departments" column, adding the name of the department. This can help other overseers and their assistants save time and effort contacting numerous volunteers.

Please note that some brothers that are trained/assigned in the Audio-Video Department are not available before and during the session, and likely will have limited availability after the session. Please consider this when double-booking these brothers as volunteers.
